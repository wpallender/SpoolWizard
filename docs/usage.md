# User Guide

## Adding a Spool

1. Open the SpoolWizard tab.
2. Enter:
   - Brand
   - Material
   - Color
   - Total Weight
   - Remaining Weight
3. Click **Save Spool**.

## Editing

Click **Edit**, modify the information, then click **Update Spool**.

## Active Spool

Click **Use** to make a spool active.

The active spool appears at the top of the inventory and is shown in the Active Spool section.

## Deleting

Click **Delete** and confirm the prompt.
